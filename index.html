About Me

Hello! This is Thien Liu. I currently live in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. As a self-taught software engineer, with over 10 years of experience, I’m a curious and motivated learner. Being an avid learner, I hold various certifications from Coursera, Google (Professional Cloud Architect), and a higher diploma in computer science. However, my passion for learning is actualized when trying to solve complex technical problems. I’ve developed dozens of web and mobile applications as a result.

I have decided to strengthen my computer science background by joining this MSc program. Our world is moving from ownership to access, and from markets to networks. Programming has never been so important. Completing my MS in Computer Science at the University of Essex would help me create a future for myself. The goal is to future-proof my career by building products and services that the world needs.
